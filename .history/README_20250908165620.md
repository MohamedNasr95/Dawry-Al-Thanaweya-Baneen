# Dawry-Al-Thanaweya-Baneen

### Game Rules:
    01. Players have 2 matches per week.
    02. Matches will be played with best of 3 system.
    03. Round consists of 10 points.
    04. Players have to choose and accept 2 Judges for the match.
    05. Both players have to accept judges decisions. 
    06. Judges must focus on stepping on the line.
    07. Every player plays 2 matches with every opponent
    08. Every player has 1 time out per round, and an extra time out on deuce.
    09. last 2 players in the season won't participate in the next season.
    10. Any point stoped due to a random player crossing or Interferes is replayed.
    11. Cheering is accepted even while points are played.
    12. 
    
### Points distribution:
    1. Winning a match 2-0 >> 2 points.
    2. Winning a match 2-1 >> 1 point.
    3. Losing a match      >> 0 points.

### Positions:
    1. Positions are determined based on points.
    2. If 2 players are same in points then, we determine position based on rounds won.
    3. If 2 players are same in rounds won then, we determine the position based on points gained.
    4. If 2 players are same in points gained then, they play a match to decide the position.

## Prohibitions:
    1. If 2 players agreed on making one of them win, both are disqualified.
    2. If a player give up a match, both players are disqualified.
    3. If a player performed carelessly in any match, player is disqualified.
    4. If a player started a match when he is tired, both players are disqualified.
    5. If a judge cooperated with a player, both are disqualified.
    6. If a player intercepts another match on purpose
